Transaction Currency

Currency of Transactions:

All transactions processed on www.ardelistech.com and www.iriss.ai are conducted in Euro (€). The use of Euro as our standard transaction currency simplifies the payment process for our customers located in the Eurozone and other parts of the world familiar with this currency.

Payment Processing:

We have partnered with Stripe, a leading online payment processing platform, to securely handle all financial transactions. Stripe supports a wide range of payment methods, ensuring a seamless checkout experience for our customers.

Currency Conversion:

Customers making purchases from outside the Eurozone may be subject to currency conversion fees applied by their card issuer or bank. The final amount charged by your bank or card issuer may vary due to these conversion rates, and Ardelis Technologies has no control over these rates or fees. We recommend consulting with your bank or card issuer if you have any questions regarding currency conversion or associated fees.

Price Display:

All prices on our website are displayed in Euro (€). For your convenience, you may use our currency converter tool (if available) to estimate the cost in your local currency. Please note, this is an estimate only, and the actual charge will be in Euro (€).

Secure Transactions:

Security is paramount at Ardelis Technologies . Stripe's advanced security measures ensure that your payment information is encrypted and securely processed without any compromise to your data privacy.

Customer Support:

Should you have any questions regarding payment, currency conversion, or any aspect of your transaction, please do not hesitate to contact our customer support team at admin@ardelistech.com. We are here to assist you every step of the way.
