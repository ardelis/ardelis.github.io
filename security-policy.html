Security Capabilities and Policy for Transmission of Payment Card Details

Commitment to Security:

At Ardelis Technologies, we take the security of your personal and payment information seriously. Our commitment to data security is designed to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your information throughout the transaction process.

Secure Payment Processing with Stripe:

We have partnered with Stripe, a global leader in online payment processing, to securely handle your payment card details. Stripe is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1, the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry. This means that your payment information is protected by the highest standards of security.

Encryption and Data Protection:

Encryption in Transit:

All information transmitted between your device and our servers, including payment card details, is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL)/Transport Layer Security (TLS) technology. This ensures that your data is secure and inaccessible to unauthorized parties during transmission.

Encryption at Rest:

Stripe also encrypts your payment information when it is stored on their servers, providing an additional layer of security for your sensitive data.

No Storage of Payment Card Details:

Ardelis Technologies does not store your credit or debit card details on our servers. Once you enter your payment information, it is directly transmitted to Stripe for processing. This approach minimizes the risk of your payment details being exposed from our system.

Compliance and Audits:

Our website and Stripe regularly undergo security audits and compliance checks to ensure adherence to industry best practices and regulatory requirements. These efforts help to maintain a secure environment for processing your transactions.

Monitoring and Prevention:

We continuously monitor our systems for potential vulnerabilities and attacks. In partnership with Stripe, we implement measures to prevent unauthorized access and protect against security threats.

Customer Responsibility:

While we take every measure to ensure the security of your transaction, it's also important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Ensure that you sign off when finished using a shared computer and never share your personal account details with others.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our security practices or if you suspect any issues with a transaction, please contact us immediately at admin@ardelistech.com. Your security and trust are our top priorities.
