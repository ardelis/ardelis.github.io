Consumer Data Privacy Policy

Introduction:

At Ardelis Technologies, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to ensure your data is protected.

Information Collection and Use:

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of personal information we collect include:

Contact Information:

Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.

Payment Information:

We partner with Stripe to process payments. While we do not store your credit card details on our servers, Stripe collects payment information securely to process transactions.

Usage Data:

Information on how you navigate our website, including page views, access times, and browsing habits. This data helps us improve our website's user experience.

How We Use Your Information:

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To process transactions securely and efficiently.
  • To improve our website and tailor it to your preferences.
  • To communicate with you about your account, orders, and our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing and Disclosure:

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information except in the following cases:

Payment Processing:

Your payment information is shared with Stripe, our payment processing partner, under their privacy policy and security measures.

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with legal proceedings or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Ardelis Technologies, our customers, or others.

Data Security:

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • Encryption of data transmitted to and from our website.
  • Regularly updating our systems and security practices.
  • Partnering with Stripe, which adheres to the highest industry standards for data security.

Your Rights:

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right to opt-out of future marketing communications.
  • The right to complain to a data protection authority about our collection and use of your personal information.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies help us to improve and analyze our website and services. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Consumer Data Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at admin@ardelistech.com.
