Refund and Dispute Policy

At Ardelis Technologies, we are committed to ensuring the satisfaction of our customers. We understand that there may be instances where you need to request a refund or file a dispute. Our Refund and Dispute Policy outlines the processes and conditions under which refunds and disputes are handled.

Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds:

Refunds may be requested within 14 days of the purchase date. Products must be returned in their original condition, unopened, and unused. For digital products or services, refunds are only eligible if the service has not been consumed or accessed. To initiate a refund, customers are required to provide proof of purchase and, if applicable, a reason for the refund request.

Processing Refunds:

Upon receiving a refund request, our team will review the details and inform you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original method of payment within 7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your card issuer's policies.

Non-refundable Items:

Certain items may be exempt from refunds, such as: Digital products or services that have been accessed or consumed. Sale items (if applicable). Custom or personalized orders.

Dispute Policy:

Filing a Dispute:

If you believe there has been an unauthorized transaction or other discrepancies with your purchase, you are encouraged to contact us directly at admin@ardelistech.com to resolve the issue. Disputes should be reported within 21 days of the transaction date.

Resolution Process:

Our team will investigate the dispute by reviewing transaction records and any communication related to the purchase. We aim to resolve disputes amicably and efficiently, with a focus on fair outcomes for both parties. Customers are encouraged to provide all relevant information and documentation to facilitate the resolution process.

Chargebacks:

If a dispute escalates to a chargeback, we will cooperate with the payment processor (Stripe) to resolve the matter based on their policies and procedures. We reserve the right to contest chargebacks that we believe to be unjustified.

Contact Information:

For any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy, please contact us at admin@ardelistech.com.
